Holistic Thinking with MAAT

Holistic thinking integrates multiple perspectives and long-term effects using the five MAAT principles: Harmony (H), Balance (B), Creative Power (S), Interconnectedness (V), and Respect (R). Use the builder on the right to generate structured prompts that teach LLMs to reason holistically.

Key Domains

  • ✅ Ethical AI Governance — fairness in social systems.
  • ✅ AI in Medicine — automation with human-centered care.
  • ✅ AI in Smart Cities — sustainable urban management.
  • ✅ AI in Creativity — art, music, and innovation aligned with ethics.

Implementing Holistic Thinking in AI

  • • Integrate MAAT into models — self-evaluation + ethical refinement.
  • • Multi-domain learning — recognize long-term, system-level effects.
  • • Dynamic ethical weighting — context-aware, culturally sensitive values.
